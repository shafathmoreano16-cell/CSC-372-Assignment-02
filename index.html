Introduction

This page recommends three food spots near campus that are good for students. The purpose is to help someone quickly pick a place to eat without overthinking it. Each restaurant includes a main photo, a short description, and three dish cards with images. The layout is responsive so the cards stack nicely on smaller screens.

Restaurant Showcases

Don Japanese Restaurant

Location: 423 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403
Specialties: donburi, teriyaki, ramen
Hours: Mon-Fri 11am-3:30pm, 5pm-10pm; Sat 11:30am-10pm; Sun CLOSED

Don Japanese Restaurant is a cozy eatery located just a short distance from campus. Known for its delicious donburi bowls, savory teriyaki dishes, and flavorful ramen, it's a favorite among students looking for a quick and satisfying meal.

Spicy Ramen

A spicy bowl of ramen with pork and vegetables.

Hibachi Steak and Shrimp

Hibachi steak and shrimp served with fried rice.

Chicken and Shrimp Lo Mein

Chicken and shrimp lo mein with a flavorful sauce.

The Upper Deck

Location: 123 Campus Ave, Greensboro, NC 27403
Specialties: burgers, sandwiches, salads
Hours: Mon-Fri 11am-9pm; Sat 12pm-9pm; Sun 12pm-5pm

The Upper Deck is a popular spot on campus known for its casual atmosphere and delicious food. It's a great place to grab a quick bite or meet up with friends.

Traditional Wings

Crispy, seasoned wings that are perfect for sharing.

Chicken Sandwich

A grilled chicken sandwich with pickles and special sauce.

Loaded Fries

Loaded fries with cheese, bacon, and special sauce.

Kazoku Japanese Restaurant

Location: 948 Walker Ave, Greensboro, NC 27403
Specialties: sushi, sashimi, tempura
Hours: Tues-Sat 11am-10pm; Sun 12pm-9pm; Mon CLOSED

Kazoku Japanese Restaurant serves authentic Japanese cuisine. Known for fresh sushi, sashimi, and tempura dishes, it’s a favorite for campus diners.

Shrimp Tempura

Crispy shrimp tempura served with dipping sauce.

California Roll

Crab, avocado, and cucumber — great for beginners.

Spicy Tuna Roll

A bold, spicy tuna roll with fresh ingredients.
